# EFT journey — house style (canonical)

Every script in `scripts/` must match this exactly. It is the format an AI
narrator and the Speechify renderer (`tools/speechify-tts.mjs`) both depend on.

## Shape
- `# Title` — evocative, not clinical.
- `## A Continuous EFT Journey for [Theme] — [N] Rounds`
- A spoken **intro** (~10–14 lines): gentle grounding/breathing guidance, mixing
  spoken prose with bracketed cues, ending on a line about the listener's "only
  job in this moment", then `[Long pause.]` and `[Begin tapping.]`.
- **20 rounds** (≈5,000 words total), each separated by the `⸻` divider.
- A **closing** release/choose sequence, ending on the signature couplet:
  `I am still here.` / `And I am okay.`

## Brackets — the core rule
Everything that is an **instruction or a pause goes in `[square brackets]`**;
everything spoken aloud stays plain.

- Bracket: the nine point labels (`[Side of Hand]`, `[Eyebrow]`, `[Side of Eye]`,
  `[Under Eye]`, `[Under Nose]`, `[Chin]`, `[Collarbone]`, `[Under Arm]`,
  `[Top of Head]`), and every cue: `[Pause.]`, `[Long pause.]`, `[Take a breath.]`,
  `[Breathe in slowly.]`, `[Place a hand on your heart.]`, `[Begin tapping.]`,
  `[Take three slow breaths.]`, `[Say these words slowly, with meaning:]`, etc.
- Do **not** bracket: the title/subtitle, the round headings (`ROUND 1 — …`),
  the spoken intro guidance, the `Even though…` set-up lines, the reminder
  phrases, or the closing affirmations.

## Each round
- Nine points in this fixed order: Side of Hand → Eyebrow → Side of Eye →
  Under Eye → Under Nose → Chin → Collarbone → Under Arm → Top of Head.
- **Side of Hand** carries two set-up lines: `Even though [difficulty], I
  [choose / accept / honour / allow]…`
- The other eight points each get **two** short, distinct spoken lines.
- After the round, 3–5 bracketed cues (pauses + one varied breath/grounding/
  noticing instruction). Vary these between rounds.

## The arc (across all 20 rounds)
1. **Acknowledge** (early): tell the truth about what hurts; name it in the body.
2. **Soften / open** (middle): compassion; the younger self, the old story, the
   need beneath the feeling; loosen the grip.
3. **Reframe / install** (later): choose a kinder, truer story; install the new
   state; let it settle. Move gradually — don't jump to "all better" by round 3.

## Voice & rules
- Warm, unhurried, intimate — like speaking to someone you love on a hard day.
- **British English** (recognise, honour, realise, colour, practise).
- No repetition: each point's two lines differ; each round explores a new facet.
- No medical/clinical claims, no diagnosis, no SUDS (0–10) rating. This is gentle
  continuous EFT for reflection and wellbeing, not treatment.

See `scripts/01-being-present.md` for a reference example to match.
