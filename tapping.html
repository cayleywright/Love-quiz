Tapping Journeys

Gentle continuous EFT (tapping) sessions to listen to or read - for calm, presence, and feeling a little more at home in yourself.

New to tapping? EFT is gentle fingertip tapping on a few acupressure points while you say words that name what you feel and slowly soften it. Press play and follow along, tapping each point as it's named - or just read the words. For reflection and wellbeing, not medical treatment.
For fun and reflection, not diagnosis. Nothing here is saved or sent.
