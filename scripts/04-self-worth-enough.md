# I Am Already Enough

## A Continuous EFT Journey for Self-Worth — 20 Rounds

Before you begin, take a slow, gentle breath in, and let it leave you without any effort at all.

[Pause.]

You don't have to fix anything right now. You don't have to become anyone different. You are allowed to arrive exactly as you are, tired or tender or unsure, and still be welcome here.

[Pause.]

Let your shoulders drop a little from wherever they've been holding. Unclench your jaw. Let your hands rest soft and open.

[Long pause.]

This is a journey about worth — about that quiet, aching question of whether you are enough. We are not going to argue with that question or shout it down. We are going to sit beside it, gently, and slowly remember something truer.

[Pause.]

There is nothing to earn in the next while. Nothing to prove. No one is grading you.

[Pause.]

Your only job in this moment is to keep yourself company, breath by breath, tap by tap, with as much kindness as you can find.

[Long pause.]

[Begin tapping.]
