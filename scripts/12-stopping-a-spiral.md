# Stepping Off the Spiral

## A Continuous EFT Journey for Stopping a Spiral — 20 Rounds

Before you begin, take a slow, gentle breath in, and let it go without rushing. You may have come here mid-fall, with your thoughts already moving fast, one tumbling into the next. That is alright. You do not have to slow them all at once. You only have to begin.

[Pause.]

Notice that you have, somehow, found a single quiet moment to be here. The spiral was loud, and yet you reached for this. That reaching matters more than you know.

[Pause.]

You are not here to argue with your thoughts. You are not here to win against them. You are here to step off the wheel they are turning on, just for a while.

[Long pause.]

Let your feet press into the floor. Feel the chair or the ground holding you up. It is holding you up right now, without any effort from you.

[Pause.]

The tapping you are about to do is a way of giving your hands something to do and your mind somewhere softer to land. Each tap is a small interruption, a gentle hand on the runaway thought, saying — not now, not like this.

[Pause.]

You do not need to believe the calming words yet. You only need to say them and tap. The settling comes later, on its own, the way a stirred glass of water clears when you simply stop stirring it.

[Pause.]

Your only job in this moment is to follow my voice and let your hands move. Nothing else is required of you. Not solving, not fixing, not deciding. Just this.

[Long pause.]

[Begin tapping.]
