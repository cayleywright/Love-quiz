# Letting the Good In

## A Continuous EFT Journey for Happiness & Gratitude — 20 Rounds

Before you begin, take a slow, gentle breath in, and let it out without rushing, as if you have nowhere else to be.

[Pause.]

There is no right way to feel as you begin. You don't have to arrive here happy. You only have to arrive.

[Pause.]

Maybe joy has felt far away lately. Maybe the days have run together, grey and flat, and you can't quite remember the last time something made you light up inside.

[Long pause.]

That's alright. We're not going to force anything today. We're simply going to make a little room — room for the good to be noticed, room for it to be felt, room for it to be let in.

[Pause.]

As you tap, let your voice be soft. Let the words land however they land. Some will fit perfectly, and some won't, and both are fine.

[Take a slow breath.]

[Pause.]

Feel your feet on the floor. Feel the weight of your own body, held by whatever is beneath you.

[Pause.]

Your only job in this moment is to be honest, and to be kind to yourself while you are.

[Long pause.]

[Begin tapping.]
