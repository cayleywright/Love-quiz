# Setting Down What I've Carried

## A Continuous EFT Journey for Letting Go & Forgiveness — 20 Rounds

Before you begin, take a slow, gentle breath in, all the way down to the base of your belly, and let it go without rushing it out.

[Pause.]

You don't have to fix anything today. You don't have to decide anything, or be ready for anything. You only have to be here, breathing, while we begin to set something down.

[Pause.]

There is something you have been carrying. Maybe for a long time. Maybe so long that you have forgotten what your arms feel like empty. We are not going to wrench it away from you. We are going to loosen your grip on it, one finger at a time, gently, until you are ready to put it down on your own.

[Long pause.]

Let your shoulders drop a little from your ears. Let your jaw unclench. Notice the weight of your body in the chair or on the floor, held by something steady underneath you.

[Pause.]

Wherever you feel the old hurt living in you — your chest, your throat, the pit of your stomach — just let it be there. You don't have to argue with it. You don't have to make it leave.

[Pause.]

Your only job in this moment is to keep breathing, and to be kind to the one who has carried so much for so long.

[Long pause.]

[Begin tapping.]
