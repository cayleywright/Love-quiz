# Settling the Storm Inside

## A Continuous EFT Journey for Calm From Anxiety — 20 Rounds

Before you begin, take a slow, gentle breath in, all the way down past your chest, into your belly, and then let it out even more slowly than you took it in.

[Pause.]

There is nothing you need to fix right now. There is nowhere you need to rush to. You have arrived in this moment, and this moment is asking nothing of you except your company.

[Pause.]

If your mind is moving quickly, that is alright. You do not have to slow it by force. We will simply tap, and breathe, and let it find its own way down.

[Long pause.]

Let your shoulders drop a little, away from your ears. Unclench your jaw, even slightly. Let your hands rest where they fall.

[Pause.]

Breathe in slowly through your nose.

[Pause.]

And breathe out, longer than you breathed in, as though you were setting something heavy down on the ground.

[Long pause.]

The anxiety you are carrying is welcome here. We are not going to argue with it or shame it. We are going to meet it, gently, the way you would steady a frightened animal — not by grabbing it, but by being calm beside it.

[Pause.]

Your only job in this moment is to keep breathing, to keep tapping, and to let the words land however they land.

[Long pause.]

[Begin tapping.]
