# Meeting the Day From Steady Ground

## A Continuous EFT Journey for Morning Grounding — 20 Rounds

Before you begin, take a slow, gentle breath in, the kind of breath that arrives before you have even decided to take it. Let the air find the bottom of your lungs, and then let it go without effort.

[Pause.]

You have only just woken, or perhaps you have been awake a while already, lying here with the day waiting on the other side of the morning. There is nowhere you need to rush to in the next few minutes. This time belongs to you.

[Pause.]

Notice the surface beneath you. The bed, the chair, the floor under your feet. Something is holding you up, and it does not require anything from you in return.

[Long pause.]

The day has not started yet, not really. It is still soft at the edges. And you do not have to meet all of it at once.

[Pause.]

So let your shoulders come down from wherever they have crept overnight. Let your jaw unclench, just slightly. Let the morning be a beginning, and not a deadline you have already missed.

[Pause.]

Your only job in this moment is to breathe, and to tap, and to let yourself arrive gently into the day that is waiting for you.

[Long pause.]

[Begin tapping.]
