# Calm, Safe, and Open to Good

## A Continuous EFT Journey for Calm, Safety & Positivity — 20 Rounds

Before you begin, take a slow, gentle breath in, and let it out without rushing, as if you have nowhere at all to be but here.

[Pause.]

There is nothing you have to do in the next little while except breathe, and tap, and let yourself be gently looked after.

[Pause.]

Maybe you've arrived a little wound up, a little tender, a little braced. That's alright. You don't have to be calm to begin. We're simply going to settle, together, one breath at a time.

[Long pause.]

Let your shoulders come down from wherever they've been holding. Let your jaw soften. Let your hands rest open and easy.

[Take a slow breath.]

[Pause.]

Feel the ground beneath you, holding all of your weight, asking nothing of you in return.

[Pause.]

Your only job in this moment is to let yourself be here, softly, and to be kind to yourself while you settle.

[Long pause.]

[Begin tapping.]
