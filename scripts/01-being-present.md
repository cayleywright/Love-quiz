# Coming Home to This Moment

## A Continuous EFT Journey for Being Present — 20 Rounds

Before you begin, take a slow, gentle breath in, and let it out without trying to make it perfect. There is nowhere you need to rush off to right now. For these few minutes, the only place you have to be is exactly where you already are.

[Pause.]

Let your shoulders come down from wherever they have been hiding. Let your jaw unclench, just a little. You have been carrying so much, holding so many threads at once, living several steps ahead of your own life.

[Pause.]

You don't have to keep doing that here. Notice the surface beneath you, holding your weight, asking nothing of you in return.

[Long pause.]

Feel your hands. Feel the air on your skin. Feel the simple, quiet fact that you are alive, in a body, in this room, on this ordinary, irreplaceable day.

[Pause.]

We are going to tap gently together, and as we do, we are going to come home — out of the noise in your head and back into the warmth of this moment.

[Pause.]

Your only job in this moment is to be here, softly, one tap at a time. Nothing to fix. Nothing to finish. Just here.

[Long pause.]

[Begin tapping.]

ROUND 1 — EVERYWHERE BUT HERE

[Side of Hand]

Even though my mind is everywhere but here — in yesterday, in tomorrow, in a hundred imagined rooms — I choose to gently acknowledge where I really am.

Even though I have been living almost everywhere except this single moment, I accept myself, scattered and tired and trying.

[Eyebrow]

[Side of Eye]

[Under Eye]

I miss so much while my attention is off somewhere else.

[Under Nose]

[Chin]

[Collarbone]

I do not have to gather it all up at once.

[Top of Head]

Toward the one place that has been waiting for me all along.
