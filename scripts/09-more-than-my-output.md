# More Than What I Produce

## A Continuous EFT Journey for When You Feel Like a Failure — 20 Rounds

Before you begin, take a slow, gentle breath in, and let it go without rushing it anywhere. You do not have to fix yourself today. You do not have to earn the next few minutes. You are allowed to simply arrive.

[Pause.]

Settle into wherever you are sitting. Let your shoulders come down from wherever they have been holding. Let your jaw soften. Let the back of your neck be heavy.

[Pause.]

You have come here carrying a verdict about yourself. A hard one. The kind that sits behind your ribs and follows you from room to room. We are going to hold it together, gently, without arguing with it yet. Just holding.

[Long pause.]

Notice your hands. Notice your breath moving in and out, doing its quiet work, without you producing a single thing for it.

[Pause.]

There is nothing to achieve in the next twenty rounds. There is no output here. There is only you, breathing, allowed to be exactly as you are.

[Pause.]

Your only job in this moment is to tap, and to let the words move through you like weather — some of them landing, some of them passing on by.

[Long pause.]

[Begin tapping.]
