# Coming Back From Survival Mode

## A Continuous EFT Journey for Returning to Safety — 20 Rounds

Before you begin, take a slow, gentle breath in, and let it out without forcing anything. You don't have to relax. You don't have to do this perfectly. You only have to be here, with me, for a little while.

[Pause.]

You have been carrying something heavy for a long time. Longer than anyone around you may have realised. You have been bracing, and watching, and getting through — and some part of you has not properly rested in a very long time.

[Pause.]

So let's not rush this. There is nowhere to get to. We are simply going to start sending a quiet message, again and again, to the part of you that has been on guard for so long.

[Long pause.]

Notice where you are sitting. Feel the surface underneath you holding your weight. You don't have to hold yourself up right now. Something else is doing that.

[Pause.]

If your shoulders are up near your ears, you don't have to drop them. Just notice they're there. If your jaw is tight, you don't have to soften it. Just notice. We are not going to force anything loose today. We are going to invite it.

[Pause.]

Your only job in this moment is to keep breathing, and to tap gently along with my words. That's all. Nothing more is being asked of you.

[Long pause.]

[Begin tapping.]
